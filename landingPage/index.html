A Future saturated with useless data

In a world where useless files, duplicate photos and obsolete e-mails saturate digital devices, resources are being depleted at an alarming rate. This invisible digital pollution consumes energy and accelerates the waste of resources.

Fortunately, a courageous group of mice, known as the Guardians of the Cache, are mobilizing to combat this digital chaos. Their mission: to clean up devices, recycle data and restore a sustainable digital world.
